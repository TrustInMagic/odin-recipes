Coconut Rice Bowl

Description

The beauty of this Coconut Rice Bowl is that you can adapt it to seasonal veggies and use whatever protein you prefer!

Here we are using tofu as our protein along with asparagus and peas in honor of Springtime.

Ingredients

  • Jasmine rice
  • Coconut milk
  • Protein: tofu
  • Vegetables: asparagus, green onions, peas
  • Coconut oil
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the rice
  2. Make the Sauce
  3. Pat tofu dry and cut into thick strips
  4. Sear Tofu

  6. Combine
